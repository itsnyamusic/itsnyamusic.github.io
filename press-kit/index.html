The Album

NYAVERSE is NYA's first full album. Everything since 2020 has been building toward it: glitchy hyperpop production, rap and singing, German and English mixed in the same song. Written late at night, and it sounds like it.

Before the album came the singles "Denk Wieder an Dich" (May 2026), "Messer" (April 2026), "BEDWARS" (September 2025) and "SWEET MONSTER ENERGY" (February 2025), all with official videos, all streaming everywhere.

“i make music for the hours when you should be asleep but aren't.” — NYA
About NYA

NYA makes music for the hours when you should be asleep but aren't.

She's a hyperpop artist from Herten, Germany. She raps and sings in German and English, sometimes switching mid-verse, over production that's loud, glitchy and too much on purpose. The lyrics say what's real even when it's ugly.

No industry polish, no fake hype. Raw sound for people who feel too much and sleep too little. Some songs sound like a 3am voice memo, some like a club falling apart. Usually both at once.

It's the kind of music that sounds like someone finally saying the thing you couldn't say yourself.

Still awake. Still writing. Still loud.
