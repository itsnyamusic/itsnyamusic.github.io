NYAVERSE

the debut album · out 24.07.2026 · self-released

NYAVERSE is the debut album by German hyperpop artist Nya, self-released on July 24, 2026.

Across 13 tracks, Nya moves between German and English over glitchy hyperpop, rap and alt-pop production. The album touches on addiction ("Suchtproblem"), dating apps ("Tinder"), the aftermath of a toxic relationship ("Denk Wieder an Dich") and self-doubt ("Not Made To Make It").

The album was preceded by the singles "Sweet Monster Energy" (February 2025), "BEDWARS" (September 2025), "Messer" (April 2026) and "Denk Wieder an Dich" (May 2026). It features Naeyiwu on "Tinder" and "Olympia", and Mhhh7887 and Vain on "99 Problems". The closing track, "Duality2", references Duality, the 2021 EP Nya debuted with.
